🎯 POP FLY MISSIONS
👀 Track the Ball +20 XP
Toss a ball straight up 10 times. Watch it the whole way up AND all the way back down. Catch it each time!
💡 Tip: Keep your eyes on the ball like a Pokémon Trainer locked onto a battle!
🏃 Move Your Feet! +25 XP
Have someone throw 10 pop flies to different spots. Focus on moving your feet fast to get under the ball BEFORE it arrives.
💡 Tip: Get there early — like arriving at the Pokémon Center before you need it!
☀️ Sun Blinder Challenge +30 XP
Practice catching 5 pop flies while using your glove to shade the sun. This is a CENTER FIELD secret weapon!
💡 Tip: Use your glove hand like a visor. Pro move right here!
💪 THROWING MISSIONS
🎯 Target Practice +20 XP
Throw 15 times at a target (a wall, fence, or a bucket). Try to hit the same spot 3 times in a row!
💡 Tip: Step toward your target as you throw — like lunging in a Pokémon battle!
🔁 Quick Release Drill +25 XP
Catch a toss, then throw it back as FAST as you can 10 times. Pretend you're throwing a runner out at home plate!
💡 Tip: As soon as you catch it, move your arm back — no waiting!
💥 Long Bomb! +35 XP
Practice throwing from deep center field distance (ask a grown-up to help set up). Throw 8 times — aim for accuracy over power!
💡 Tip: Use your whole body, not just your arm. Rotate like a spinning top!
🌟 BONUS MISSIONS
🧘 Breathe & Focus +15 XP
Before practice, do 5 deep breaths. Breathe in for 4 counts, out for 4. This powers up your FOCUS stat!
💡 Tip: Even pro players use breathing to calm down before a big catch!
⚡ Outfield Sprint +20 XP
Sprint from one foul line to the other 4 times. Fast feet = fast fielder! Time yourself and try to beat your record!
💡 Tip: Pump your arms — your legs follow where your arms go!
